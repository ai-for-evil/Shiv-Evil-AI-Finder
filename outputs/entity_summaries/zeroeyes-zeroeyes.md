## Evidence

- ZeroEyes. ZeroEyes is a AI-powered gun detection technology founded in 2018 by a team of Navy SEALs. The system integrates with existing security cameras to monitor video feeds 24/7, scanning for visible firearms. If a firearm is detected, the system sends human-verified alerts within 3-5 seconds to school administrators and law enforcement. The technology has been widely adopted in schools across nearly 40 U.S. states.
